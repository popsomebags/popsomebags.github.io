Website Evaluations

Cybersole.io

Cybersole is a company that sells software that allows for easy purchase of multiple limited edition shoes. The website includes a dashboard, list of features, supported websites, and contact information.

  • Contrast: The website uses very good contrast. Green and black are very easy to see, and match the logo.
  • Repetition: Repeition is seen on the website. The website consistently uses the same animations as well as the same color scheme throughout.
  • Alignment: This website is very well aligned. The margins are all good and are not too close to any of the four sides.
  • Proximity: All of the information is seperated in different groups. Each section is seperated by a border.

LINGssCARS.com

LINGssCARS is a car leasing website owned by Ling Valentine. Ling claims to be Britains biggest individual seller of new cars. The website includes a menu on the left, alongside many photos of cars that are available for lease.

  • Contrast: This website has a mix of good and poor contrast. For the photos of the cars, the text pops out, but some links on the lefthand menu have poor contrast. The text below some of the images of the cars also have poor contrast.
  • Repetition: Repetition can be seen on the website. The website uses the same flashy and bright colors all around.
  • Alignment: The margins of the website are fine for the top and left, but the right menu is a little close to the border. The main issue is the bottom, which contains an absurd amount of whitespace.
  • Proximity: The proximity on this site is terrible. Everything is too close to each other, and all the information is given to you in such a small space that it is almost impossible to tell what you are looking at.
